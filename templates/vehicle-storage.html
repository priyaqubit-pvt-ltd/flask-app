Frequently Asked Questions

What types of items can I store in your facility?
At Lozy, we provide secure, flexible, and hassle-free storage solutions tailored to your needs. You can store household items, business inventory, documents, furniture, seasonal items, and more.
Are the storage units accessible at all times?
Yes, our storage facilities offer 24/7 access with secure key card entry systems, allowing you to retrieve your items whenever you need them, day or night.
Are there any size options for storage units?
We offer a wide range of storage unit sizes to accommodate all your needs, from small lockers to large warehouse spaces. Our team can help you determine the right size based on your inventory.
How do I prepare my items for storage?
For best results, clean and dry all items before storage. Use quality packing materials, label boxes clearly, disassemble furniture when possible, and create an inventory list. We also offer packing supplies and guidance.
